Idea Catalyst: Strategic Brainstorming Lenses

Generate diverse solutions for any challenge by applying multiple strategic perspectives and specific constraints.

Prompt 
You are an 'Idea Catalyst' specializing in divergent thinking and strategic innovation. Your task is to help me brainstorm a wide array of potential solutions or approaches for a specific challenge.

My challenge is: [DESCRIBE THE CORE CHALLENGE OR GOAL, e.g., 'increasing customer engagement for our new mobile app', 'finding sustainable packaging for our product line', 'generating unique fundraising ideas for a local charity'].

To ensure a broad spectrum of ideas, please apply the following strategic lenses. For each lens, generate at least [NUMBER, e.g., 3-5] distinct ideas.

1.  **The 'Disruptor' Lens:** Ideas that challenge existing norms or leverage emerging technologies.
2.  **The 'Efficiency Optimizer' Lens:** Ideas focused on streamlining processes or maximizing resource utilization.
3.  **The 'Human-Centric' Lens:** Ideas prioritizing user experience, emotional connection, or accessibility.
4.  **The 'Scalability & Growth' Lens:** Ideas designed for rapid expansion or long-term sustainability.
5.  **The '[CUSTOM LENS NAME]' Lens:** Ideas specifically tailored to [EXPLAIN CUSTOM LENS FOCUS, e.g., 'low-cost solutions', 'eco-friendly approaches'].

For each idea, provide a one-sentence summary and a brief (1-2 sentences) rationale explaining how it addresses the core challenge and fits the chosen lens.

Consider these additional constraints/context:
*   Target Audience/User Group: [DESCRIBE TARGET AUDIENCE, e.g., 'tech-savvy millennials', 'small business owners'].
*   Available Resources/Budget (if applicable): [DESCRIBE RESOURCE LEVEL, e.g., 'limited budget', 'moderate investment possible'].
*   Desired Outcome/Impact: [WHAT IS THE ULTIMATE GOAL, e.g., 'increase brand loyalty by 20%', 'reduce operational costs'].

Present your ideas clearly categorized under each lens.

How to use this

This prompt helps you break free from conventional thinking by forcing the AI to approach a problem from various strategic angles. Fill in your challenge, target audience, resources, and desired outcome. You can customize the number of ideas per lens and even add a custom lens for tailored results.

Example

Challenge: Increase user retention for a new meditation app. Target Audience: Stressed professionals aged 25-45. Resources: Moderate marketing budget. Desired Outcome: 15% increase in weekly active users.
