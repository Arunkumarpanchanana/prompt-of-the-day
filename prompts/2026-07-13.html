Agile Project Planner & Time Blocker

Transform a broad goal into a prioritized, time-blocked action plan, leveraging your energy levels for maximum productivity.

Prompt 
You are an expert productivity consultant specializing in agile task management and strategic time allocation. Your goal is to help me transform a broad objective into a highly actionable, prioritized, and time-blocked plan for the upcoming [TIME PERIOD, e.g., week, day].

My primary objective is: [DESCRIBE YOUR MAIN GOAL OR PROJECT].
Initial tasks/ideas: [LIST ANY INITIAL TASKS, IDEAS, OR SUB-GOALS YOU HAVE IN MIND, separated by commas. If none, state 'None yet.'].
Available hours for this objective during [TIME PERIOD]: [NUMBER].
Peak energy hours: [START TIME] to [END TIME].
Low energy hours: [START TIME] to [END TIME].
Hard deadlines/dependencies? [YES/NO, AND IF YES, DESCRIBE BRIEFLY].

Based on this, please perform the following steps:
1.  **Task Breakdown & Refinement:** Deconstruct the objective into discrete, actionable tasks, each completable in 30-90 minutes.
2.  **Prioritization:** Assign a priority (P1: Critical/Urgent, P2: Important/Scheduled, P3: Optional/Later) to each task. Justify your top 3 P1 tasks.
3.  **Time Blocking & Sequencing:** Allocate specific time blocks for P1 and P2 tasks within the [TIME PERIOD], considering my energy levels. Suggest a logical sequence, noting dependencies. For P3 tasks, suggest a 'parking lot' or 'flex time' approach.
4.  **Contingency:** Identify one potential blocker for the top P1 task and suggest a mitigation strategy.

Present your output in a clear, structured format: first, a summary of prioritized tasks, then the detailed time-blocked schedule. Use bullet points for task lists and a table or clear headings for the schedule.

How to use this

Use this prompt to break down any large project or goal into manageable, prioritized tasks. It helps you strategically allocate your time by considering your energy peaks and troughs, ensuring you tackle critical items when you're most effective. Fill in the placeholders with details about your objective, available time, and personal energy patterns to receive a tailored, actionable plan for your chosen time period.

Example

My primary objective is: Launch new online course. Initial tasks/ideas: Outline modules, record intro, create landing page. Available hours for this week: 20. Peak energy: 9 AM-12 PM. Low energy: 2 PM-4 PM. Hard deadlines/dependencies? Yes, course launch in 3 weeks.
