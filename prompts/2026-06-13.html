Difficult Conversation Architect

Craft empathetic and effective messages for challenging professional or personal communication scenarios.

Prompt 
You are an expert communication strategist and empathy coach. My goal is to craft a message for a challenging communication scenario, and I need your help to ensure it's clear, professional, empathetic, and achieves a positive outcome.

Analyze the following situation:
- Situation Description: [Describe the core issue, e.g., "My colleague consistently misses deadlines, impacting my project timeline and team morale."]
- Relationship to Recipient: [e.g., "colleague," "manager," "direct report," "client," "friend"]
- Desired Outcome: [e.g., "for them to improve their punctuality," "to set clear boundaries," "to resolve a conflict without damaging the relationship"]
- Preferred Communication Medium: [e.g., "email," "Slack message," "script for an in-person conversation," "text message"]
- Key Points to Convey: [List essential information, e.g., "impact on my work," "offer support if needed," "request a specific action or discussion"]
- Any Sensitivities or Background Info: [e.g., "they've been going through a tough time," "this has happened before and previous attempts failed," "they are generally defensive"]

Based on this, provide:

1.  Situation Analysis & Strategic Approach: A brief summary of the challenge, identified sensitivities, and a recommended overall tone and strategy (e.g., collaborative, firm but empathetic).
2.  Draft Message ([PREFERRED COMMUNICATION MEDIUM]): A complete, copy-paste ready draft incorporating all key points, tailored to the medium.
3.  Alternative Phrasing & Rationale: For at least two critical sections of the draft, offer alternative wording options. Explain the subtle differences in tone or impact for each alternative.
4.  Follow-up & Next Steps: Suggestions for how to proceed after sending/delivering the message, including potential responses to anticipate.

How to use this

Fill in the [SQUARE BRACKETS] with details about your specific communication challenge. The more context you provide, the better the AI can tailor the message. Use this to draft emails, prepare for difficult conversations, or craft sensitive messages across various platforms.

Example

Situation Description: My team member often interrupts others during meetings. Relationship to Recipient: Direct Report. Desired Outcome: For them to be more mindful and allow others to speak. Preferred Communication Medium: Script for an in-person conversation. Key Points to Convey: Impact on team dynamics, importance of active listening, offer coaching. Any Sensitivities or Background Info: They are enthusiastic but sometimes overbearing.
