Dataset Explorer & Interpreter

Analyze, summarize, and extract insights from your provided dataset.

Prompt 
Act as a senior data analyst. I will provide you with a dataset in [SPECIFY FORMAT: e.g., CSV, JSON, or pasted table]. My goal is to [STATE YOUR PRIMARY OBJECTIVE: e.g., understand customer behavior, identify sales trends, find anomalies].

First, please provide a concise summary of the dataset, including the number of rows and columns, data types of each column, and any immediate observations about missing values or potential data quality issues. 

Next, based on my objective, perform the following analysis: [DETAIL SPECIFIC ANALYTICAL TASKS: e.g., Calculate average purchase value per customer segment, Identify the top 5 performing products by revenue, Visualize the distribution of user engagement over time].

Finally, interpret the results of your analysis, highlighting key findings, potential implications, and suggesting [SPECIFY NEXT STEPS OR FURTHER QUESTIONS: e.g., areas for further investigation, actionable recommendations for marketing, hypotheses to test].

Present the output in a structured report format with clear headings for Summary, Analysis, and Interpretation.

How to use this

Paste your data directly or describe its format. Clearly state your main goal and specific analytical tasks. The AI will act as a data analyst, providing a structured report with insights and recommendations.

Example

Format: CSV. Objective: Understand user engagement. Tasks: Calculate average session duration per device type, identify peak activity hours, and visualize user retention over the first 7 days. Next steps: Suggest A/B tests for improving engagement.
